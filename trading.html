Trinity

Your cryptocurrencies Personal assistant.

About Trinity

Trinity is a bot created with educational purposes only, This bot and the information provided by it are just estimations and mere speculations. This project is not intended to give financial advice in no way whatsoever.

I created this bot to get all the information i required in just one place. In order to communicate with the bot as easy as possible, an interface with telegram was created with the address @raulfftbot. By using different commands (e.g. /about), one can communicate with the bot and have access to its different functionalities:

  • Get the coins with the estimated higher projection in the short term .
  • Find the most recent news about any topic.
  • Get the most recent Tweets about any topic.
  • Get the most recent stats(volume, market cap, etc.) from any coin on Coincap.io.
  • and more...

How do the predictions work

The bot was trained using different Machine Learning and Deep Learning models trained with open source data gathered from Bitfinex's API. This models were trained to find out of all coins are the ones that have the higher projection in the short term. The predictions displayed by the bot are the ones with the highest accuracy score and can go up to 80% accuracy on the validation data. This information is not financial advice.
